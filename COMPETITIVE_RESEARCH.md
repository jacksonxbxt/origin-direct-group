# Competitive Research: Keyword Rankings & Competitor Weaknesses
**Date: 2026-03-23**

---

## Executive Summary

After analyzing 10 target keywords and 30+ competitor websites, the opportunity is massive. Most African commodity supplier websites suffer from the same critical weaknesses: no pricing, no product specs, no FAQ schema, thin content, and zero thought leadership. The B2B marketplace listings (Alibaba, TradeWheel, Go4WorldBusiness) dominate many searches but are generic and impersonal. A focused, content-rich site with proper SEO can outrank them within 3-6 months on long-tail keywords and 6-12 months on head terms.

## Content Templates for Product Pages

Each product page should include:
1. **Hero section** with product image and key stats
2. **Product overview** (200-300 words)
3. **Specifications table** (grades, moisture, purity, packaging)
4. **Pricing section** with bulk tiers and "Request Quote" CTA
5. **Origin & sourcing story** (150-200 words, with photos)
6. **Use cases / applications** section
7. **Certifications & quality assurance**
8. **FAQ section** (10-15 questions with FAQPage schema)
9. **Related products** internal links
10. **CTA** -- Request quote / Contact

**Target word count per product page: 1500-2500 words**
