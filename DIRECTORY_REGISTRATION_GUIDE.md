## Tips for Maximum Impact

### 1. Use Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone)
Always use the exact same company name, address, and phone across ALL platforms. Google uses NAP consistency as a ranking signal.

### 2. Product Listings > Just a Profile
On platforms that allow individual product posts (Go4WorldBusiness, TradeKey, EC21, ExportHub, TradeFord), post **at least 5-10 individual products** each with:
- Clear product title (e.g., "Raw Unrefined Shea Butter - Bulk Export from Ghana")
- 200+ word description with keywords
- 3-5 product images
- MOQ, packaging, delivery terms
- Price range or "Contact for Quote"

### 3. Prioritize Verification
Verified/trusted supplier badges dramatically increase inquiry rates. Complete verification on:
- TradeKey (Business Verification)
- Tridge (Company Verification)
- Kompass (auto-verified in 24-48h)
- Go4WorldBusiness (document upload)

### 4. Images Matter
Prepare a folder with:
- Company logo (high-res PNG, transparent background)
- Banner/cover image (1200x400 px)
- Product images for each commodity (minimum 3 per product)
- Certifications/quality documents (if available)

### 5. Backlink Monitoring
After registering on all platforms, wait 2-4 weeks, then check which profiles are indexed by Google:
```
site:go4worldbusiness.com "Origin Direct Group"
site:tradekey.com "Origin Direct Group"
site:ec21.com "Origin Direct Group"
site:kompass.com "Origin Direct Group"
site:europages.com "Origin Direct Group"
site:crunchbase.com "Origin Direct Group"
```

### 6. Don't Forget to Link Back
On your origindirectgroup.com website, consider adding a "Find Us On" or "Partnerships" page that links to your profiles on these platforms (not required for backlinks, but good for trust signals).
