# Hookah / Shisha Charcoal Buyer ICP Research
*Compiled: March 2026*

---

## ICP Overview

Four distinct buyer profiles exist in the hookah charcoal market:

| ICP | Description | Key Needs |
|-----|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Charcoal brand / OEM (private label) | Manufacturer, consistent quality, custom shapes/branding |
| 2 | Hookah product distributor – Middle East | Bulk pricing, halal cert, container volumes |
| 3 | Hookah product distributor – Europe / US | Compliance certs, steady supply, retail packaging |
| 4 | Large shisha lounge chains | Regular supply, good burn, competitive price |

## Key ICP Targeting Summary

### Highest Priority (ready to buy, clear procurement need):

1. **Titanium Charcoal (Prime Hookah Inc.)** — already importing, own brand = clear OEM buyer
2. **BLACKCOCO's GmbH** — pure brand, sources from Indonesia, Germany HQ, expanding to US
3. **ASA Europe GmbH** — Europe's largest shisha distributor, already buying Carbopol (can pitch alternative)
4. **Shisha Wholesalers** — #1 US hookah wholesale distributor, documented importer
5. **Hookah Wholesalers / business.hookah.com** — Al Fakher parent company, high volume
6. **El-Badia Pro** — French market gatekeeper since 1991, B2B wholesaler
7. **Naara Charcoal (Australia)** — private label brand, actively seeks suppliers

### Outreach angles by ICP:

- **ICP 1 (OEM brands):** "We can supply 25mm cube / custom shape at X $/kg, minimum 2 tons, OEM packaging — same factory specs as your current supplier"
- **ICP 2 (Middle East distributors):** "Container pricing, halal cert, GCC compliance docs, 12–25 day shipping from Indonesia"
- **ICP 3 (Europe/US distributors):** "EU/US compliance certs, consistent quality, competitive vs. current Indonesian suppliers, sample before commit"
- **ICP 4 (Lounges):** "Bulk direct pricing, no minimum after first order, lounge box format (10kg)"
