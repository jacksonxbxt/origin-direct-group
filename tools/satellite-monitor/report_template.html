Data Sources & Methodology

Satellite weather data: NASA POWER API — Prediction of Worldwide Energy Resources. Provides daily precipitation, temperature, and humidity data at 0.5-degree resolution, with ~5 day lag.

ENSO monitoring: NOAA Climate Prediction Center — Oceanic Nino Index (ONI) for El Nino/La Nina classification.

Methodology: Current-month rainfall is compared against 30-year climatological averages from NASA POWER. Deviations exceeding 30% trigger monitoring alerts; deviations exceeding 50% trigger severe alerts. Price impact estimates are based on historical supply-price relationships for each commodity and should be treated as directional guidance, not precise forecasts.

Indicative prices: Reference prices are approximate FOB/origin values for estimation purposes. Actual contract prices depend on quality, volume, delivery terms, and negotiation.
